About me

When I graduated with a Mathematics degree from Trinity College Dublin, I sat down and thought about what I really wanted to do. Not your average mathematician, I didn't want to be tied down to just a career in finance and figures, so I decided to broaden my search. A desire to be creative coupled with a love for writing code led me to set forth down the web development path.

My academic background has equipped me with strong technical skills and the ability to pick anything up quickly. This follows through into my work; you can expect well written code that follows best programming practices and translates into smoothly functioning sites. Merging this with an understanding of good design principles and a constant hunger to learn more leads to elegant websites that run like a dream.
