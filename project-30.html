Deliberately making a difference.

I want my job to be more than a means to support my family; I want to make a difference in our world with my daily work. I'm very conscious that to make a meaningful difference in any given industry, it requires a degree of specialization. You can't drive innovation when you don't deeply understand the field you're working in.

In 2024 I turn 30. I'm giving myself until then to learn, research, and study the different areas that have the potential to drive positive and impactful change in our societies while also piquing my interest. I've identified 5 areas, listed below, that I want to focus my efforts and attention on, with the end goal of choosing an industry to devote a significant chunk of my professional career to.

Maybe it'll take less than two years, maybe it'll take more. That doesn't matter. What matters is that despite the progress we've made as humanity, there is more to do. We need more justice, more equality, and less suffering, for both our planet and our people. I want my life to make other's lives better, and this is a baby step towards fulfilling that goal. More to follow!
